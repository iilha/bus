Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

Overview

Taiwan Bus ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Feedback submissions: When you submit feedback, we collect your message and optional email address if you choose to provide it for replies.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Browser UUID: A unique identifier stored in your browser's local storage to distinguish sessions
  • Usage data: App version, platform (web/Android/iOS), screen size, device pixel ratio
  • App state: Current city selection, active tab (for feedback context)
  • Location data: Your location is only accessed when you use the "Nearby Stops" feature and is never stored or transmitted to our servers

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the Taiwan Bus service
  • Respond to your feedback and support requests
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Debug technical issues
  • Analyze app performance and usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your preferences (language, city selection) and browser UUID are stored locally on your device
  • Cache: Route data is cached locally for 24 hours to improve performance
  • Server Storage: Feedback submissions are stored securely on our servers
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party tracking or advertising services

Third-Party Services

Taiwan Bus uses the following third-party services:

  • TDX API: Taiwan's Transport Data eXchange API for real-time bus data
  • OpenStreetMap: Map tiles for displaying bus stops and routes
  • Leaflet.js: Open-source mapping library

These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your personal information with these services beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

Your Location Data

When you use the "Nearby Stops" feature:

  • Your browser will request permission to access your location
  • Location data is used only to find nearby bus stops
  • Your location is never stored or transmitted to our servers
  • You can revoke location permission at any time in your browser settings

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored on your device until you clear browser data
  • Feedback: Retained for up to 2 years for support and improvement purposes
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for location access
  • Clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser storage

Children's Privacy

Taiwan Bus does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
